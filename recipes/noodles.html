Ching's Secret Instant Noodles

Description

The main part of this recipe is the Ching's Secret Instant Noodle packet. However, we will spice up this recipe. We will add a variety of spices to highten the flavor of the instant noodles. The recipe is perfect if you're on a time crunch and most of the ingredients are ingredients that most indian households have.

Ingredients

  • Ching's Secret Instant Noodles (Any Flavor)
  • 1.5 tsp of Canola Oil
  • 2 Serrano peppers
  • 3/4 Large Onion
  • 1 tsp of Red Chili Powder
  • 1 tsp of Cumin Powder
  • 1/4 tsp of Turmeric

Steps

  1. Put wok on stove on medium heat
  2. Put 1.5 tsp of Canola Oil
  3. Cut the peppers and mince the onions
  4. Once the oil is heated, put peppers and onions
  5. Once the onions are brown, put Red Chili Powder, Cumin Powder, and Turmeric to taste
  6. Put 4 cups of water
  7. Put all the masala packets
  8. Break all the noodle packets in half and put them in
  9. Turn gas on low
  10. It's done when the noodles are cooked
